# Tankly Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 18, 2025**

## Overview
Tankly ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection
Tankly collects and stores the following data locally on your device:
- Daily water intake records
- Achievement and progress data
- App preferences and settings
- Unlocked items and mystery creatures

## Data Storage
All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage frameworks. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers.

## Apple Health Integration
If you enable Apple Health integration:
- Your water intake data is synced with the Apple Health app
- This data is subject to Apple's Health app privacy policy
- You can disable this integration at any time in Settings

## Game Center
If you use Game Center features:
- Your scores and achievements are submitted to Apple's Game Center
- Your Game Center profile and data are subject to Apple's Game Center privacy policy
- This is optional and can be disabled in your device settings

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data remains on your device.

## Children's Privacy
Tankly does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

## Data Deletion
You can delete all your data at any time using the "Reset All Data" option in the app's Settings.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.
